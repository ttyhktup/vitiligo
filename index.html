NEWS

In 2023, by the decision of the Board of Directors of the Vitiligo Research Foundation (VR Foundation, USA) city ​​of Almaty, Kazakhstan, is declared the international headquarters of the 13th World Vitiligo Day (World Vitiligo Day, abbreviated as WVD).

CARE NOT ONLY ABOUT BEAUTY, BUT ALSO ABOUT HEALTH

The dermatological clinic "Vitiligo and skin health Centre" employs high-class professionals with extensive experience and excellent results in practical activities. Our priority areas are innovative methods of treatment of skin diseases: vitiligo (phototherapy, transplantation of non-culturable cellular elements), psoriasis, and skin neoplasms (nevi, papillomas, molluscum contagiosum, keratomas, warts). Comprehensive and qualified treatment with specialists - helminthologist and endocrinologist will help you find the beauty and health of your skin!

MAIN DIRECTIONS

Treatment of endocrine diseases

  • Hyperthyroidism;
  • Hypothyroidism;
  • Cushing's disease;
  • Addison's disease;
  • Acromegaly;
  • Diabetes;
  • Thyroiditis.

Treatment and therapy of skin diseases

  • Vitiligo;
  • Psoriasis;
  • Atopic dermatitis;
  • Eczema;
  • Acne;
  • Skin neoplasms: nevi, molluscum contagiosum, papillomas, keratomas, warts.

Treatment of parasitic diseases

  • Lamblias;
  • Roundworm;
  • Bovine tapeworm;
  • Pork tapeworm;
  • Broad tapeworm;
  • Balantidia intestinal;
  • Lancet fluke;
  • Cat fluke;
  • Pinworms.
